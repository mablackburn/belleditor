WORKSHOP: MEASURE MAPPING
• Map: Top-left of measure to Bottom-right.
• Quick Add: Click the [+] next to active measure.
• Resize: Select measure, drag Right or Bottom edge.
• Calibrate: Drag purple lines to align.
• Orange Handles: Indicates a manually locked beat.
• Subdivide: Hover exactly between two beats to add a subdivision.
• Select Zone: Click top or bottom of a measure.
• Snap & Click: Hover near beat lines to place a note.
• Select & Edit: Click an existing note to edit it.
• Shortcuts: Acc = S/F/N/A | Len = W/H/Q/E/X | Tech = R/L/M/K/P | Toggle Damp = D
• Pan: Right-Click drag or Space+Drag.
